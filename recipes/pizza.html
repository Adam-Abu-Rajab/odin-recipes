Pizza Recipe

Home

Description

pizza, dish of Italian origin consisting of a flattened disk of bread dough topped with some combination of olive oil, oregano, tomato, olives, mozzarella or other cheese, and many other ingredients, baked quickly

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (can be store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce (pizza sauce or marinara)
  • Cheese (mozzarella is the most common)
  • Olive oil (optional, for brushing the crust)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C) or as hot as your oven allows. A hot oven gives a crispy crust.
  2. Roll out the dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Transfer the dough to a pizza pan or baking sheet. If you have parchment paper, use it to prevent sticking.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small border for the crust.
  5. Sprinkle cheese generously on top of the sauce.
  6. Brush the crust with olive oil (optional, for a golden crust).
  7. Bake for about 10-15 minutes or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is bubbly.
